Find everything you need about renewable energy!

3D platform Avares is a virtual world in 3D. Here, you will create your own avatar, with which you will go towards the knowledge of all renewable energy alternatives.

In this world you get to know the main aspects of different types of renewable energy.

3D world is divided into six islands, each inviting you to travel. Your journey will begin on the main island, which offers an introduction to the 3D world, including instructions for its use. Here you can discover various details about the project, its initiators, and from here you can then move to any other island.

From the main island you can go and discover the islands of renewable energies: solar energy, hydro energy, wind energy, geothermal energy and biomass energy.

Begin the journey into the world of 3D!

1. To start your journey into the world of 3D, it is necessary to sign up here.

2. Download and install OpenSim viewer (preferably Koku). Button to download is below.

3. Open the viewer and go to [Grid Manager -> Grids -> Add New Grid]. Enter the grid aigroup.ceid.upatras.gr:9000 and press OK.

4. Log in to viewer by using credentials from the first step. (Username - Firstname Lastname)

Now, take advantage of renewable energy world in 3D!
